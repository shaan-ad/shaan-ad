## Why I keep building this

AI tools are built for engineers. The people who need them most are the ones
leading teams, making decisions, and running operations. Every project here
closes that gap, whether it's giving PMs an AI copilot, making agent-building
accessible, or bringing observability to AI workflows.

If you lead a team and wish your tools were smarter, something here was probably
built for you.
